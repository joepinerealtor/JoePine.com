Important Note

Some search actions may work better on the full KW site.

Some Keller Williams and IDX setups allow a search page to stay embedded cleanly, while others limit what can happen inside an iframe.

In practice, that means one of two things will happen:

  • The iframe works, and visitors can browse the embedded search while staying inside your site shell.
  • The iframe is blocked or partially limited, and visitors should use the full-search button for the best experience.
If Keller Williams gives you an official IDX embed snippet or widget, that is usually the cleanest long-term option for this page.
