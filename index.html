About Joe

Experience, innovation, and true dedication to your goals.

Joe Pine is a seasoned REALTOR with Keller Williams Leading Edge | Platinum Real Estate Group, with more than five years of experience helping clients buy, sell, and invest across Rhode Island and Massachusetts. Licensed in both RI and MA, Joe proudly serves buyers and sellers across a broad Rhode Island and Massachusetts market area, stretching west to Coventry, north to Worcester, east through Bridgewater and Mattapoisett, and back through Southeastern Massachusetts and Rhode Island's East Bay, covering the many communities in between. Based in North Providence, he pairs local market knowledge with a tech-savvy approach to create a smoother, more informed real estate experience.

Known for responsiveness, integrity, and results, Joe approaches every transaction as if it were his own and does everything possible to guide it to a successful closing. He is passionate about smart home technology, modern real estate tools, and staying ahead of the trends that help clients make better decisions.

Outside of real estate, Joe enjoys riding motorcycles, relaxing with friends and family, and spending time with his cats, Milo and Luna. My signs are on the move, are you?
