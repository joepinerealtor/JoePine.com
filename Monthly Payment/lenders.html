Step 1 | Get Preapproved

Choose the lender that fits your move, your timeline, and the states where you plan to buy.

Preapproval is where your affordability estimate becomes more real. A lender can review income, debts, credit, assets, and loan options so you know what may truly be possible before you start writing offers.

This page is set up to make lender options easy to compare. Each card shows who the lender is, how to reach them, where they are licensed, and where to start the preapproval conversation.

What to Ask
