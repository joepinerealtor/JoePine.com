Search Wrapper

Keep visitors inside your site while they browse your KW search page.

This page wraps your main Keller Williams search experience inside `JoePine.com` so visitors can preview listings and keep your branding around the search.

If your KW/IDX provider allows framing, visitors can browse the embedded search directly below. If it blocks embedding, they can still open the live search in a new tab with one click.

Important Note

Embedding may depend on KW or IDX restrictions.

I wasn’t able to fully verify frame permissions from the command line because the KW site is protected by a JavaScript and cookie challenge before the search page loads.

Browser security also limits how much one website can control another website inside an iframe. That means this page is ready for a real browser test, but one of two things will happen:

  • The iframe works, and visitors can browse the embedded search while staying inside your site shell.
  • The iframe is blocked by KW/IDX frame rules, and we should switch to their official embed widget or a direct-link preview experience instead.
If your Keller Williams website gives you a specific IDX embed snippet, script, or widget code, that is the best option. I can drop that in and make it look native to your site. Without that, cross-origin iframe navigation controls may be limited by browser security rules.
